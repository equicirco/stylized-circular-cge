# Single-Country Manuscript Notes

These are notes for later manuscript preparation, not executable model
specification.

## Interpretation Limits To Document Later

- The current model is a closed single-country equilibrium model, so it cannot
  represent international leakage or distribution between consuming and mining
  countries.
- Government revenue is recycled as a representative-household transfer. The
  model does not represent alternative public spending compositions.
- Distributional results are functional and sectoral: activity, labor-use, and
  capital-use reallocations across sectors. They are not household-class
  distributional results.
- Employment and aggregate output should be interpreted as closure-dependent
  diagnostics, not robust headline outcomes.
- Short-run transition dynamics, unemployment, and demand-constrained adjustment
  would require a different closure.
